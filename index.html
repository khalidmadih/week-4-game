Crystals Collector

Game Rules

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals and each represent a value. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Good luck!!
